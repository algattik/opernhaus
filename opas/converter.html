Opas Data to Calendar Converter

Instructions:

  1. Open Opas and log in.
  2. Navigate to `Kalendar` and then select the option `meine Termine`.
  3. Open the 2024-2025 season data. (For other time ranges, adjust the dates appearing in the URL).
  4. If "unlogged" is displayed, go back to step 1 to log in.
  5. This displays technical data in the browser. Copy all the data shown (Ctrl-A, Ctrl-C on PC; Command-A, Command-C on Mac) and paste it in the box below.
  6. Click the button to generate the calendar and Excel files.
